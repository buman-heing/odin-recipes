Rice

Description

Rice is a delicious starch filled meal, which makes it great for whatever time of the day

You do not have to be Gordon Ramsey to figure out how to cook this, plus we will be here to guide you through it.

Ingridients

  • Rice(5kg)
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Boil some water in a kettle
  2. Make a mix of cold water and Rice, with a water to malie meal ratio of 2:1
  3. When the water boils, mix the water with the mix and let it simmer
  4. Make sure to continously stir until the mix starts to boil
  5. Close the lid and check on the rice every five minutes and taste it to see if it is ready
