Nshima

Description

Nshima is a delicious starch filled meal, which makes it great for whatever time of the day

You do not have to be Gordon Ramsey to figure out how to cook this, plus we will be here to guide you through it.

Ingridients

  • Mealie Meal(5kg)
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Boil some water in a kettle
  2. Make a paste with cold water and mealie meal with a water to malie meal ration of 2:1
  3. When the water boils, mix the water with the paste and let it simmer
  4. Make sure to continously stir until the Paste thickens
  5. When the paste is thick enogh, slowly add more mealie meal and continue to stir
  6. When the pase becomes hard enough, close the lid for 10 mins and voila
