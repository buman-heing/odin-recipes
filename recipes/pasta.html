Pasta

Description

Pasta is a delicious starch filled meal, which makes it great for whatever time of the day

You do not have to be Gordon Ramsey to figure out how to cook this, plus we will be here to guide you through it.

Ingridients

  • Pasta(5kg)
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Boil some water in a kettle
  2. Make a mix of pasta and water with a ratio of 1:2 respectively
  3. When the water boils, mix the water with the paste and let it simmer
  4. Make sure to continously stir until the Pasta softens
  5. Add spices and water to taste
